Simple Macaroni and Cheese

Quick,easy, tasty macaroni and cheese dish. Fancy designer mac and cheeese costs forty or fifty dollars to prepare when you have so many exotic and expensive cheeses,
but they arent always the best tasting. This simple recipe is cheap and tasty.

Ingredients for Mac and Cheese

  • 1 box of macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded chedar cheese

How to make it:

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. To make the roux, start by melting butter in a saucepan over a mediun heat.
    Add flour, salt and pepper and stir until smooth
  3. Add cheese and macaroni
